The Concept

A mini-festival

Live music! a field! A Barbeque! REAL beer!

Basically, we wanted a celebration that was not yer typical wedding do - so we hired a big tent, a band and a barbeque for a party in a field...

As well as the Bimble Inn, we are using Wessex Marquees for additional bad weather cover.
