Honeymoon

We went to the West Coast of Scotland for our honeymoon. We went to Oban some years ago, and loved it, so we came back...

Our house

We had a lovely little old fishermans cottage on the shore, just south of Campbelltown. It was wonderfully peaceful. We could see seals from our front door...

There's also some random pictures of stuff we saw and liked.

Machrihanish Sealife observatory

Otherwise known as the shed... A fairly remote observatory for birds and sealife. We spent a good few hours here - basically taking photos of a lazing seal. You can find out more at their website at http://www.machrihanishbirds.org.uk/

Kildonan Dun

A prehistoric habitation site. Very picturesque with stunning views across to Arran. The site itself is well maintained also. Check out the Campbelltown website for more details.

Saddell Abbey

The ruins of an old abbey with some interesting graves and some displayed tomb covers. See Campbelltown website for more details

Carradale waterwheel

There is a goldsmith here as well.

Skipness Castle

A fantastic old castle ruin. For more info see the castle website.

The Seafood Cabin

Fantastic food! With ducks!

Tarbert

A fishing village with a castle overlooking. Well, the earthworks of anyway...

Kilberry stones

Some medieval carved tomb covers.

Scottish Owl Centre

Owls... Excellent handling and flying demonstration. Great views of Campbelltown as well. We just went nuts and got pictures of just about all the owls they have...

Visit their website at www.scottishowlcentre.com.

Westport Beach

A surfing beach - white sand and rolling breakers.

Glasgow Royal Botanical Gardens

The name says it all really... Lots of cool plants. Oh yes, and a squirrel climbing up my leg.
