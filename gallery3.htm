Machrihanish Sealife observatory

Otherwise known as the shed... A fairly remote observatory for birds and sealife. We spend a good few hours here - basically taking photos of a lazing seal. You can find out more at their website at http://www.machrihanishbirds.org.uk/

Kildonan Dun

A prehistoric habitation site. Very picturesque with stunning views across to Arran. The site itself is well maintained also. Check out the Campbelltown website for more details.

Saddell Abbey

The ruins of an old abbey with wome interesting graves and some displayed tomb covers. See Campbelltown website for more details

Carradale waterwheel

A fantastic old castle ruin. For more info see the castle website.
