The Concept

A mini-festival

The original concept was to have a pagan style celebration that incorporated a recognisable marriage ceremony which, importantly, was conducted by a blacksmith. We wanted a blacksmith because of the cultural significance of such a key figure in hisorical village life. Many of you will already know about the blacksmith at Gretnor Green, bu we also wanted to incorporate our own ceremony and celebration into the whole event...

We found a fantastic celebrant in Joe, who is a genuine working blacksmith, and a poet as well! We could not have found a better person to conduct the ceremony and you can see from the ceremony pictures what we mean.

Everything else followed from this. We found a fantastic primary venue in The Bimble Inn, and location to match.

Have a look at the galleries to see what we mean...

The whole thing went of brilliantly, due in no small part to the huge help of various people who can be seen on our thanks page.
